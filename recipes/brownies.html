Brownies

Description

These fudgy, delicious brownies are inspired by the viral recipe from Alvin Zhou. This recipe has recieved over 8.1 million views from his Youtube and have been duplicated numerous times to test and see if it is worth spending 100 hours making a batch of brownies. Regardless, it is hard to argue how delicous the final product looks! An example of a Youtube channel that has replicated his viral recipe is none other than Tasty. A channel boasting more than 21.3 million in subscriber count.

Ingredients

  • Butter (both unsalted and melted)
  • Eggs
  • Sugar (caster and brown)
  • Cocoa powder
  • Espresso powder
  • Vanilla extract or paste
  • Salt
  • Chocolate (both 75% dark chocolate and milk chocolate)
  • All-purpose flour

Steps

Brownie Batter Preparation

  1. Brown the butter: Melt butter in a saucepan and cook until it turns golden brown, developing a nutty flavor.
  2. Incorporate flavorings: Stir in espresso powder (and potentially a coffee ice cube to cool it down).
  3. eggs and sugar: Beat eggs with sugar and vanilla until light and fluffy.
  4. Combine dry ingredients: Sift together flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  5. Mix wet and dry: Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet, mixing until just combined.
  6. Add chocolate: Fold in melted chocolate (and chocolate chips or chunks, if desired).

The Long Chill:

  • Refrigerate for 72 hours:Pour the batter into a baking pan (lined with parchment for easy removal) and cover it tightly. Refrigerate for 3 days (72 hours).

Baking and Further Chilling

  1. Preheat oven: the oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Bake: Bake the brownies for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  3. Cool and chill: Let the brownies cool completely in the pan, then cover and refrigerate for another 24 hours.
  4. Serve: After the final chilling, the brownies can be cut and enjoyed.
