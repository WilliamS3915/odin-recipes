Chicken Alfredo

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to make your own very delicous and healthy chicken alfredo. This meal is an all time favorite and is sure to leave you feeling well fed and happy. The recipe is simple and can even be used for meal prep for the week!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parsley (optional)

Steps

Chicken Preparation

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a bit of oil.
  3. Cook the chicken breasts for about 6-7 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked through and have a nice golden-brown color.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing it into thin strips.

Fettuccine Pasta Preparation

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Add the fettuccine pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta and set it aside.

    4. Alfredo Sauce Preparation

    1. In the same skillet used to cook the chicken, melt the butter over medium heat.
    2. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant.
    3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer.
    4. Reduce the heat to low and gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan cheese until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
    5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
    6. Add the cooked fettuccine pasta to the skillet and toss it in the Alfredo sauce until well coated.
    7. Add the sliced chicken on top of the pasta.
    8. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
    9. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade Chicken Alfredo!
